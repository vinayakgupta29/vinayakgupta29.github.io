This is My Universe.

Hey. You’ve found the one-stop shop for my entire brain. This place isn't meant to be a polished portfolio or a neatly organized blog. If you’re here looking for something that makes perfect, linear sense, you might be in the wrong place. But if you’ve ever felt like your thoughts are a tangled mess of bright threads, all demanding your attention at once—art, music, writing, and a thousand half-baked ideas—then you might feel right at home. This website is a direct reflection of my ADHD and Autistic mind. For me, life is a kaleidoscope of hyperfixations and special interests, a constant, beautiful, and sometimes overwhelming hum of creative energy. I don’t experience the world in neatly categorized folders; I experience it as a whirlwind of sensory input, a symphony of ideas that often collide, and sometimes create something surprisingly wonderful in the chaos. This is a place where a snippet of a poem can sit right next to a satirical take on modern life, where a link to my Twitch stream is a heartbeat away from a piece of my digital art. It’s not disorganized, it’s organized according to a logic that only makes sense inside my head—a logic of passion, impulse, and deep, focused curiosity. The disorder you see is actually a deeply personal form of order. Each "messy" card on this page is a part of me. It's not a resume; it’s a living map of what I am passionate about right now. Some of these things might stick around, while others might disappear as my focus shifts. I've learned to stop fighting the chaos and start embracing it. This site is my playground, my digital sketchbook, and my personal library all at once. It's where I can dump a random thought, showcase a completed project, and share a piece of music I'm obsessed with, all without the pressure of a professional facade. It's a safe space to be authentically me. So, feel free to poke around. Click on anything that catches your eye. There's no right way to navigate this site. There’s no predetermined path. Just let your own curiosity guide you. And if you find something you like, or if a particular piece of chaos resonates with you, don't be a stranger. Hit me up on one of the links below. You're now a part of my universe.
